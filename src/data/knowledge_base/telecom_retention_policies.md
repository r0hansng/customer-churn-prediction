# Telecom Master Retention Policies

## 1. High Monthly Charges Mitigation
Customers experiencing bill shock or whose primary churn driver is high monthly charges are eligible for the following mitigation strategies:
- **Loyalty Discount**: Apply a 15% discount for 6 months if the customer has been with us for more than 1 year.
- **Downgrade Alternative**: Recommend downgrading premium add-ons (e.g., Streaming Services, Device Protection) instead of canceling the base internet/phone plan.
- **Rate Lock**: Offer a 2-year contracted rate lock at their current or slightly reduced price to assure them costs won't rise.

## 2. Technical Issues / Poor Support Experience
If a customer is churning due to poor technical support or unresolved connectivity issues:
- **Priority Support Access**: Grant the customer 3 months of Priority Tech Support (skips the queue).
- **Free Equipment Upgrade**: If their router or modem is over 2 years old, offer a free equipment replacement.
- **Service Credit**: Issue a $50 statement credit as an apology for past disruptions.

## 3. Competitor Contract / Better Offer Segment
When a customer mentions moving to a competitor or getting a better offer elsewhere:
- **Price Match Guarantee**: Offer to match any local competitor's advertised price for 12 months.
- **Contract Buyout**: If they are looking to leave because we didn't buy out their last contract, offer a retroactive credit.
- **Free Premium Channels / Services**: Add free international calling or 3 free months of premium TV channels to increase the perceived value of our current package.

## 4. Month-to-Month High Risk Segment
Customers on Month-to-Month contracts who show signs of leaving:
- **Sign-on Bonus**: Offer a $100 loyalty bill credit if they sign a 1-year agreement today.
- **Bundle Offer**: Suggest combining Internet and Mobile lines for a 20% total bill reduction, which statistically lowers churn by 40%.

## Communication Guidelines
- Always express understanding of the customer's frustration.
- Highlight the length of the customer's relationship with the company (if > 1 year).
- Make the offer sound exclusive ("As a valued customer, I can approve a special exception...").
